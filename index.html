Event design encompasses the more creative side of event management. While a planner focuses on organization, we focus on the decor of the event. From draping to lighting, furniture, and centerpieces, it is our job to ensure your event is truly stunning.

It's never too soon to start! The time to plan events varies from a few weeks to years. If your event is small, you can probably put it together in a month or two. A good rule of thumb for any major event, from weddings to a company holiday party, is six months.

Cancellations made 7 days or more in advance of the event date, will receive a 100% refund. Cancellations made within 3 - 6 days will incur a 20% fee. Cancellations made within 48 hours to the event will incur a 30% fee.
